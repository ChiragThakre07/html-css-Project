WHO WE ARE


HOME OF HOPES is a non-profitable organization dedicated to uplifting orphaned and abandoned children by providing them with love, care, and opportunities for a better future. We believe that every child deserves a safe home, quality education, and emotional support to grow into confident and independent individuals.
